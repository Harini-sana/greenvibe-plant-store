💡 Plant Care Tips by Category

💧

Indoor Plant Watering

Most indoor plants prefer slightly moist soil. Check soil moisture by inserting your finger 1-2 inches deep. Water when the top inch feels dry.

☀️

Light Requirements

Outdoor plants generally need 6-8 hours of direct sunlight, while indoor plants thrive in bright, indirect light. Rotate plants weekly for even growth.

🌡️

Temperature Control

Keep indoor plants between 65-75°F (18-24°C). Avoid placing them near heating vents or air conditioners for consistent temperature.

✂️

Pruning & Maintenance

Regular pruning promotes healthy growth. Remove dead, diseased, or yellowing leaves promptly. Trim back overgrown branches in late winter.
