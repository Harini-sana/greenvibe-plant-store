🌿 About GreenVibe

Cultivating green spaces, nurturing plant lovers, and growing a sustainable future together since 2020

Our Growing Story

GreenVibe began as a small passion project in 2020 when our founder, a plant enthusiast, struggled to find high-quality plants and reliable care information in one place. What started as a weekend hobby quickly blossomed into a mission to make plant parenthood accessible to everyone.

We believe that every space deserves the beauty and benefits of plants. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just beginning your plant journey, GreenVibe is here to support you with carefully curated plants, expert guidance, and a community that shares your green passion.

Today, we've helped over 10,000 plant parents create their perfect green spaces, and we're just getting started!
